📍 GeoPay — How It Works

A simple step-by-step guide to getting your land surveyed and paying safely on the blockchain

🏠
Client
Land owner who needs a survey done and pays the fee
📐
Surveyor
Licensed professional who does the field survey work
🔒
Smart Contract
Holds the money safely until work is done — no middleman
🏛️
SURCON/NIS
Government body that approves the survey and receives 70%
1
📏
Enter Your Land Details
Client
Tell GeoPay how big your land is (in square meters) and whether it is private or commercial. The system automatically finds the correct official fee from the Kwara State government fee schedule.
20 official fee tiers Private or Commercial Auto-calculated
2
💰
Lock Your Payment in Escrow
Client
You pay the official survey fee in SUI tokens. The money goes into a smart contract (escrow) — think of it like a digital safe. Nobody can touch the money until the surveyor completes the job.
⏰ 7-Day Deadline starts now — the surveyor must submit the survey document within 7 days or you get your money back automatically.
STATE: PENDING Money safely locked 7-day countdown starts
3
🗺️
Surveyor Does the Field Work
Surveyor
The licensed surveyor goes to the land, measures the boundaries, and prepares the official survey plan. This must be done within 7 days of the client locking payment.
📐 The surveyor prepares the Digital Red Copy — the official survey document in DWG, PDF, AutoCAD or any format.
4
📁
Upload the Survey Document
Surveyor
The surveyor uploads the completed survey document (in any format — DWG, PDF, AutoCAD, DXF, PNG) to Walrus — a decentralized storage system. The file is stored permanently and cannot be deleted or tampered with.
🦭 Walrus is like a permanent, tamper-proof filing cabinet on the blockchain. The document stays there forever.
STATE: SUBMITTED Stored on Walrus SURCON/NIS notified
5
🏛️
SURCON Reviews & Approves
SURCON/NIS
The regulatory body (SURCON/NIS) is automatically notified when the document is uploaded. They review the survey plan to confirm it meets all official standards, then approve it directly on the blockchain.
✅ This approval is recorded permanently on-chain — it cannot be faked or altered by anyone.
STATE: APPROVED On-chain approval Client notified
6
✅
Client Confirms & Payment is Released
Client
Once SURCON approves the document, the client confirms they are satisfied. The smart contract automatically splits and sends the payment — no manual calculation, no delays, no middleman.
STATE: COMPLETED Instant payment split Zero middleman

⏰ What if the Surveyor Misses the 7-Day Deadline?
No problem! If the surveyor does not upload the survey document within 7 days, the client can claim a full automatic refund directly from the smart contract — no need to contact anyone, no delays, no arguments. The blockchain handles it automatically.
EXPIRED → Full refund automatically sent to client wallet
⚖️ What if There is a Disagreement?
1
Either the client or surveyor can raise a dispute if they are not satisfied. They explain the reason on the blockchain.
2
The job is frozen — nobody can touch the money while the dispute is being reviewed.
3
SURCON/NIS reviews the dispute fairly and decides on a custom split — for example, 50% to surveyor and 50% refund to client.
4
The smart contract automatically sends each party their agreed amount — instant and tamper-proof.
DISPUTED → SURCON resolves with fair custom split
